Academix: Digital Repository Promoting Lumil Integrated National High School's Academix Research

About the Research

This digital repository is dedicated to showcasing the various academic works and research outputs of the students of Lumil Integrated National High School. The research aims to provide a comprehensive platform for digital academic resources to be accessed by students, educators, and other stakeholders.
